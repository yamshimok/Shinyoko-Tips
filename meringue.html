Shinyoko Tips

Introduction how to enjoy, where to buy, where to have meals around Shinyokohama. Only locals know.

Meringue

What's Meringue?

It's Hawaiian restaurant. I strongly recommend you to have a pancake, which is most fluffy one you have ever had. Please NOTE that the pancake would be slow to arrive, it would take over 20 minutes.

Meringue's nearest station is Kiahine-Kouen, B24.

After getting off the train, go to exit No.2. You pass the gate, then go upward with the escalator. Then you can find the exit No.2.

On your left hand, there is Meringue.

This is the pancake, which is whipped butter pancake, I strongly recommend. I want you to see the thickness and feel how fluffy.

The menu of pancakes is shown above. There are many pancakes with various fruits sources, but I feel it donesn't need on the pancake. If it's your first time, please enjoy whipped butter pancake!

Meringue is so crowded between 10:00-13:00 on both weekday and weekend. There is the ticket vender machine at the entrance. So touch the blue button on top-right, then you will get the ticket with a number. When the number on right-buttom, 85 is shown as above picture, is same as your number, show your ticket to the staff. The red number on left of 85, 6 is shown, means how many people is waiting for at this moment.

During 8:00-11:00 of weekday, Meringue offers special breakfast. You can enjoy the whipped butter pancake and one drink with around 600 yen! It's worth to go.

They can accept cash and credit card, PASMO.
