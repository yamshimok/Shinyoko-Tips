Shinyoko Tips

Introduction how to enjoy, where to buy, where to have meals around Shinyokohama. Only locals know.

Takao

What's Takao?

It's reasonable to enjoy japanese tenpura. Here tempura are freshly fried and served one by one.

Where is Takao?

The nearest station is Center-Minami, B29.

From the exit of the station, you can see Southwood on the left hand.

Go to 2nd floor with the escalator and turn right, so you can find Takao. If you find people keep in line, please follow the end of it.

Inside Takao

There are about 10 tables can be seat in the family of four and a few small tables for couples. There are also some counter seats. In case of the counter seat, ypu can directly see the chef will fry tempura in the front of you.

Menu

The menu is shown above. You can ask to have the menu in english. In lunch time, it cost around 1000 yen, it's so reasonable.

On the table

On the table, there are lightly pickled vegetables and Mentaiko, which is spicy cod roe. You can have them freely.

You can also find two bottles of condiment paste made from yuzu zest and chili peppers. The red one is made from red pepper and the green one is made from green chili. You can try tempra dip into them.

There are also the bottle of salt and Wasabi salt.

Meet Tempra

After waiting for a few minites, the staff will bring fresh tempra on your tray one by one.

After finishing your meal, take the receipt and go to the cash register. They can accept both cash and a credit card.

Payment and sourvenir

Near the register, you can see the souvenir counter. You can buy some souvenirs there.
