Shogakuji

What's Shogakuji?

This is a famous as a hydrangeas temple because irises and hydrangeas begin to bloom in the temple every year when the rainy season approaches.

During this time, many people come to visit each year and it is very crowded even on weekdays.

If you have a plan to come Shinyokohama in early of June, I strongly recommend to go there.

Not so large temple, so it will take less than 1hr. to go around. And it's for free to entering the temple as you might know.

The name of the temple is Shokaku-ji, and the nearest station is Center-Minami, B29. From the station, it takes about 10 minutes on foot.

The gate of the temple is so japanese tasteful.

Walk the approach to the main hall. If you reach the main hall, I think you don't need any offering and praying. Only enjoy.

At the front of main hall, you will see iris on your left.

Go further inside, you will see hydrangeas.
