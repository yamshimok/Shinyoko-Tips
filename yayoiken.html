Shinyoko-Tips: Tips to enjoy Shinyokohama

Restaurant

Where to eat

Around Shinyokohama station, there are many restrants you can enjoy with cheaper. This article introduce such restaurants and explains how to seat, pay.

Yayoi-Ken

It is so reasonable and you can enjoy casual japanese food's. And you would not need speaking japanese so much.

To go to nearest YAYOI-KEN, get off at Kannai(B17) from Shinyokohama(B25) via Yokohama Subway.

You will find the ticket vending machine as soon as entering YAYOI-KEN. Push 'English' button, put enough bills and coins into blue circle of above picture at bottom, and choose what you want. Then you can get ticket and changes from yellow circle of above picture at bottom.

You can sit on any seat you want. Usually the staff will come to take your tickets and confirm orders. You don't need to order to say anything, because they will only check tickets.

If the staff doesn't seem to come over 5 minutes, please call him by the bell on the table and give your tickets.

You can have allyou-can-drink green tea and water by yourself.

After finishing dishes, you can leave there freely.

Convenient phrases to ask something

When you want to ask someone in japanese, please tap buttons below. Japanese translations would be shown. Let her to see it. It would be help you.

Where is the rice steamer?

ご飯はどこでおかわりできますか？

Give me another bottle of japanese pickles.

お漬物のおかわりをください

Where is the tea pot?

お茶はどこでおかわりできますか？

Give me another bottle of water.

お水のおかわりをください
