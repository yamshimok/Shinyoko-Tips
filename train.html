Shinyoko-Tips: Tips to enjoy Shinyokohama

Japanese mannar of geting on a train

JR Lines and Yokohama Subway Lines

At Shinyokohama station you can find two lines, JR Lines and Yokohama Subway Lines. Japanese have common manner when getting on a train of not only them but also other lines. This article explain how to buy ticket on Yokohama Subway Lines and the manner of JR Lines and Yokohama Subway Lines.

Enter/Exit ticket gate

In ShinYokohama station, you can get on both Yokohama Subway Lines and Yokohama Lines. You can pass the ticket gate by touching pink circle of above picture with PASMO. At that time, please note blue circle of above picture. This upper arrow mark (⇧) means the gate can accept touching of PASMO. If not, ured cross mark(×) will be shown, the gate would not accept any touching of PASMO. In such case, please use other gates.

When exiting the gate, you also need touching of PASMO.

Manner of Yokohama Subway Lines

At the front of each platform's door, You can wait for coming trains (see above picture on the left). While waiting, get in line like red circle in the middle of above picture. It is common scene of waiting trains like above picture on the right. Please note that you have to wait getting on the train until passengers getting off.

Manner of JR Lines

At the front of each green marks on the platform, You can wait for coming trains (see red circle of above picture on the left). While waiting, get in line like red circle in the middle of above picture. It is common scene of waiting trains like above picture on the right. Please note that you have to wait getting on the train until passengers getting off.
