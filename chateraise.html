Chateraise

What's Chateraise

Here you can enjoy various sweets and pastries ONLY to go. It is so reasonable.

Chateraise is in CenterMinami(B29) and you can get japanese sweets that is reasonable price.

Go to Kouhoku Tokyu Shopping Center and go down the stairs. Chateraise is in B1F.

Recommended Sweets

Ichogo Daifuku
Ichigo daifuku is a soft rice cake stuffed with sweet bean paste and a strawberry. It is a popular traditional Japanese sweet. "Ichigo" means "strawberry" in Japanese and "daifuku" is a soft rice cake, usually stuffed with sweet bean paste.

Yomogi Manjyu
Yomogi Manjyu is shown on the right of the picture, bean-paste bun with japanese mugwort. Yomogi Dango is shown on the left of the picture and rice-flour dumplings mixed with japanese mugwort.

Sakura Mochi
Sakura Mochi is a pink rice cake filled with sweetened bean-paste, wrapped in a salt-preserved cherry leaf. "Sakura" means "cherryblossom" in japanese and "Mochi" means a soft rice cake.

Shio Daifuku
Shio Daifuku is a soft rice cake stuffed with salt-seasoned bean paste. "Shio" means "salt" in Japanese and "daifuku" is a soft rice cake.

Karinto Manjyu
Karinto Manjyu is fried-buns containing strained adzki-beans paste with crispy dough (of brown cane sugar). "Fried dough cookies" means in japanese.

Reasonable Manjyu
Both Manjyus are sets of 10 pieces about 100 yen and rice cakes filled with sweetened bean-paste. The pink one is a pink rice cake with the smell of cherry.

Pancakes
On the left of the picture, it is the pancake with whipped cream. Another is the bean-paste pancake and called "Dorayaki" in japanese.

Castella
It is the castella with coarse sugar covering the bottom.
